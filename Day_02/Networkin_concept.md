# Topologies
>way a network is arranged, including the physical or logical description of how links and nodes are found to relate to every other.

>maps :how different nodes on a network--including switches and routers--are placed and interconnected, as well as how data flows.

>Diagramming the locations of endpoints and repair requirements helps determine the most precise placement for every node to optimize traffic flows

> easier to locate faults, troubleshoot and fixes problems, and share resources across networks.

>traffic flows circularly around a closed network loop
